A LITTLE ABOUT US

Started in 2001, Hassan Construction Company has built outstanding residency projects in Kohsar, Hyderabad. Some of our successful projects include OASIS Apartments, Beautiful Homes, Tulip Tower and Royal Residency. Following our great success in Kohsar, we have also opened a new head office in Mirpurkhas and our launching our new scheme with the name of Gateway Dreamland.
